Protected areas are crucial for the conservation of biodiversity, the preservation of ecosystems, and the protection of endangered species. In today's rapidly changing world, they play a vital role in combating climate change, protecting water resources, and ensuring sustainable livelihoods. The following websites provide information on various issues and strategies regarding protected areas globally.

  • IUCN: About Protected Areas
  • WWF: Protected Areas
  • UNEP: State of Protected Areas
  • Government of Canada: Protecting Nature
  • National Geographic: Last Wild Places
